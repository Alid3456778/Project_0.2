Taj Mahal

About :

Journey to Agra and witness the unparalleled beauty of the Taj Mahal. A masterpiece of Mughal architecture, this ivory-white marble mausoleum stands as a testament to eternal love. Admire its intricate details, symmetrical gardens, and the changing hues that paint the sky during sunrise and sunset, creating a truly magical and iconic experience.

Location:

The Taj Mahal is located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India.

Getting There

1 .By Air:

The nearest airport is the Indira Gandhi International Airport (DEL) in New Delhi, which is well-connected to major cities worldwide.
From Delhi, you can take a domestic flight to Agra's Kheria Airport (AGR).

2 .By Train:

Agra is well-connected by train. The Agra Cantt Railway Station is the main railway station in the city..

3 .By Rode:

Agra is connected by a network of well-maintained roads. You can hire a taxi, take a bus, or drive to Agra from major cities like Delhi or Jaipur.

Best Time to Visit:

The best time to visit the Taj Mahal is from October to March when the weather is pleasant.

Tips:

1 .Sunrise Visit:

Witnessing the sunrise at the Taj Mahal is a magical experience. Arrive early to avoid crowds.

2 .Security Check:

There are strict security checks at the entrance. Avoid carrying prohibited items.

3 .Explore Surroundings:

Take time to explore the Mehtab Bagh gardens across the Yamuna River for a unique view of the Taj Mahal.
